🔌 Install LeadGeeks Chrome Extension

The extension adds a "+ Add to Fida" button directly on LinkedIn search results and profiles. Select your playbook from the bar at the bottom of LinkedIn — leads queue up and auto-import into this app.

📋 Install steps:
  1. Download the zip below & unzip it to a folder
  2. In Chrome go to chrome://extensions
  3. Toggle on Developer Mode (top-right)
  4. Click Load unpacked → select the fida-extension folder
🔄 How to use it:
1 Keep this app open in one tab so the extension can sync your playbooks
2 Open LinkedIn Sales Navigator — a Fida Neo bar appears at the bottom of the page
3 Select your playbook from the dropdown in the bar
4 Click "+ Add to Fida" on any search result or profile page
5 Switch back to this app — leads auto-import into your playbook!
